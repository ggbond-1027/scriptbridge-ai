# Product

## Register

product

## Users

ScriptBridge AI serves novel authors, short drama writers, content teams, and reviewers who need to convert multi-chapter fiction into a structured screenplay draft. They work in an editing context: importing source chapters, inspecting AI analysis, correcting scenes, validating YAML, and exporting deliverables.

## Product Purpose

The product turns three or more novel chapters into a traceable, valid, editable screenplay YAML project. Success means a user can import a story, run a multi-stage adaptation pipeline, inspect the story bible and scenes, edit YAML or scene cards, validate the structure, and export YAML, Markdown, Fountain, or JSON without touching model internals.

## Brand Personality

Precise, focused, editorial.

## Anti-references

- Not a marketing landing page.
- Not a single prompt wrapper that only returns raw text.
- Not a decorative AI dashboard with vague generation progress.
- Not a dark neon developer toy.
- Not a card-heavy SaaS layout that hides the authoring workflow.

## Design Principles

1. Show the structure behind the generation.
2. Keep source text, screenplay scenes, and YAML validation visible together.
3. Treat AI output as editable draft material, not final truth.
4. Make errors actionable and local to the scene or YAML line.
5. Prefer familiar editor affordances over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Use keyboard-accessible form controls and buttons, visible focus states, reduced decorative motion, and text labels alongside critical icons.
